Vidya Poshak

Vidya Poshak, India is a registered Non Government Organization (NGO), serving education community. The organization was started by a group of compassionate people in the year 2001 at Dharwad with the aim of supporting financially challenged bright students.In all 3900 graduates have settled through gain full employment in their life and brought their families out the clutches of economic backwardness. More than 1,00,000 Youth were trained through different training program under C-Doss, ELAAP, RBC, ELIP etc. More than 20,000 youth utilize our library services every year. Students are provided Academic books, Reference books, Career counseling & Free Computer & Internet access. More than 250 Million Indian rupees invested in last 13 years for Empowerment of Youth.

    Unique Features :

  • Financial Support for students who are financially challenged
  • Vidya Poshak is one of the few Voluntary Organizations which is Assessed & Graded by CRISIL
  • Financial Support for Engineering & Medical Courses
  • Opportunity for Fresh Graduates who are looking for a Career in Corporate Sector
  • Residencial Camps for students to improve there skills

About Residential Bridge Camps

Vidya Poshak organizes 9 days Residential Bridge Camps that help students achieve optimal success during and after their college education. The RBCs aim at bringing positive change in the overall personality of the students. The camps also stress on Skills, Knowledge and Attitude apart from Career guidance and counseling (both career and personal). The camps can be of either 5 days or 7 days duration. RBCs strive to bridge the gap between the academia and career goals of students. It augments the needs of students which they rarely get in their formal schooling.
